Shanghai is a dynamic international city where tradition meets modern life. From historic streets and cultural landmarks to futuristic skylines and vibrant neighborhoods, the city offers endless possibilities for exploration, food, culture, and everyday urban experiences.

Must-See Attractions

Yu garden

Yu Garden is a classical Chinese garden located in the heart of Shanghai. Built during the Ming Dynasty, it features traditional pavilions, rockeries, ponds, and winding paths.

Disneyland

Shanghai Disneyland stands out from other Disney parks with its immersive design and unique attractions. It is home to the world's first Zootopia themed land.
